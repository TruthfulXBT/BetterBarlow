The Story

Built from the
ground up.

Hayley Barlow has spent over two decades operating at the most senior levels of UK contact centre management. Her career spans some of the country's largest and most complex operations — from multi-site outsourcers managing hundreds of client relationships, to NHS trusts, national education providers, and global BPO groups.

In January 2026, Hayley founded BarlowBetter Ltd — an interim management and consultancy practice built on a simple premise: organisations deserve honest, experienced advice from someone who has actually done the job, not just advised on it.

Her tagline says it all: Build. Fix. Grow.
