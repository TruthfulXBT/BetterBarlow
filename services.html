01
Interim Management

When your contact centre needs experienced leadership immediately — whether due to a sudden departure, a performance crisis, or a period of rapid change — BarlowBetter can step in at director or C-suite level from day one.

Unlike a permanent hire, an interim brings no political baggage, no onboarding lag, and no long-term overhead. You get senior expertise, focused entirely on your situation, for exactly as long as you need it.

Engagements typically run from 3 to 12 months and cover everything from day-to-day operational leadership to full transformation programmes.

Operations DirectorCX DirectorC-Suite CoverCrisis Management
02
CX Transformation

Customer experience transformation is about far more than improving CSAT scores. It requires a fundamental rethink of how your operation is designed, how your people are led, and how your culture shapes every customer interaction.

BarlowBetter approaches CX transformation from the inside out — starting with an honest diagnosis of where your operation actually is, not where you think it is. From there, we build a programme that sticks.

This includes journey mapping, channel strategy, complaint reduction, cultural change, and coaching leadership teams to sustain improvements long after the engagement ends.

Journey MappingCultural ChangeCSAT ImprovementChannel Strategy
03
Operational Performance

Poor operational performance in a contact centre almost always has deeper root causes than the headline metrics suggest. High attrition, poor adherence, low first contact resolution — these are symptoms. BarlowBetter finds the causes.

We conduct deep operational reviews that look at your structure, your processes, your management layers, your data, and your culture. The output is a clear, prioritised improvement plan with realistic timelines and measurable outcomes.

We can then stay to lead delivery, or hand off to your team with full support.

Operational ReviewPerformance TurnaroundProcess ImprovementWorkforce Management
04
Leadership Development

The biggest constraint on contact centre performance is usually not process or technology — it is leadership capability below director level. Team leaders and middle managers are the engine of your operation, and most are under-developed and under-supported.

BarlowBetter designs and delivers leadership development programmes that are rooted in the real world of contact centre operations. Practical, honest, and focused on building the confidence and skills that make a measurable difference.

Programmes can be delivered for cohorts or individual coaching at senior level.

Leadership CoachingTeam Leader DevelopmentSenior MentoringManagement Programmes
05
Human-Enabled AI

AI is reshaping contact centre operations rapidly, but most organisations are either moving too fast without a strategy, or too slowly out of fear. BarlowBetter helps you navigate this thoughtfully.

Our philosophy is human-first: technology should augment your people, not simply replace them. We help you identify where AI and automation genuinely add value, where they don't, and how to implement change in a way your teams can embrace.

This includes agent assist tools, automated quality monitoring, chatbot strategy, and workforce planning in an AI-augmented environment.

AI StrategyAgent AssistAutomation PlanningChange Management
06
Strategic Consultancy

Sometimes what a contact centre needs most is an experienced, independent voice at board level — someone with no agenda other than giving honest advice. BarlowBetter provides exactly that.

Strategic consultancy engagements typically involve board or senior leadership team advisory, helping organisations make better decisions on structure, investment, technology, outsourcing, and long-term direction.

Engagements can be project-based or retained, and are always grounded in real operational experience rather than theoretical frameworks.

Board AdvisoryStrategic PlanningOutsourcing StrategyRetained Advisory
